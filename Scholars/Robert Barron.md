# Robert Barron (Bishop)
*1959– · Catholic · New Evangelization*

> **Why he's here:** The most widely followed Catholic media evangelist of the 21st century. His *Word on Fire* YouTube channel has tens of millions of views; his *Catholicism* documentary series was broadcast on PBS. A serious Balthasarian-Thomist theologian who chose the terrain of popular media as his primary field.

## Key Concepts
- **Beauty as the first way** — evangelization through beauty (art, architecture, music) before argument; the via pulchritudinis
- **Participation** — all reality participates in God's being; the Thomistic/Neoplatonic vision against modern nominalism
- **Balthasarian universalism** — Barron holds the "reasonable hope" position that hell may be empty; has been criticized by traditionalists
- **New Evangelization** — John Paul II's call for re-evangelization of formerly Christian cultures

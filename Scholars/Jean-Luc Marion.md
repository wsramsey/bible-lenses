# Jean-Luc Marion
*1946– · Christian · Phenomenological Theology / French*

> **Theological lean:** God as gift, Saturated phenomenon, Idol vs icon, Beyond Being

## Hermeneutical Lenses

- [[Lens - Phenomenological]] — [Phenomenological](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phenomenology_of_religion)
  *Experience of the sacred as primary; Ricoeur, Otto, Marion*
- [[Lens - Mystical / Apophatic]] — [Mystical / Apophatic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apophatic_theology)
  *God beyond language; silence, negation, union; Gregory of Nyssa, Eckhart*
- [[Lens - Philosophical Theology]] — [Philosophical Theology](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philosophical_theology)
  *Applies philosophy to theological questions; Aquinas, Tillich, Plantinga*

## Shaped By

- [[Pseudo-Dionysius the Areopagite]] — *foundational*
  > Marion's phenomenology of givenness retrieves Dionysian apophatic theology
- [[Augustine of Hippo]] — *direct*
  > Marion's most important philosophical work is on Augustine's Confessions

- *Being Given*

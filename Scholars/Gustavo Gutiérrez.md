# Gustavo Gutiérrez
*1928–2024 · Christian · Liberation Theology / Dominican*

> **Theological lean:** Preferential option for the poor, Praxis over theory, History as locus of salvation

## Hermeneutical Lenses

- [[Lens - Liberation Hermeneutics]] — [Liberation Hermeneutics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Liberation_theology)
  *Reads from underside of history; poor, marginalized; Gutiérrez, Cone*
- [[Lens - Historical-Critical]] — [Historical-Critical](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Historical_criticism)
  *Seeks original meaning in historical context; source, form, redaction criticism*
- [[Lens - Social-Scientific Criticism]] — [Social-Scientific Criticism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Social-scientific_criticism_of_the_Bible)
  *Applies anthropology and sociology; Malina, Neyrey*

## Shaped By

- [[Paul of Tarsus]] — *direct*
  > Gutiérrez reads Paul's solidarity with the poor as central to the gospel
- [[Karl Barth]] — *formative*
  > Gutiérrez's political theology engages Barth's Christocentrism

## Influenced

- [[James Cone]] — *parallel*
  > Cone and Gutiérrez developed liberation theology simultaneously from different contexts

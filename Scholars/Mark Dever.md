# Mark Dever
*1960– · Evangelical · Church Ecclesiology*

> **Why he's here:** The founder of 9Marks Ministries and the most influential voice for Reformed Baptist ecclesiology in contemporary evangelicalism. His *Nine Marks of a Healthy Church* gave thousands of pastors a framework for reforming church culture toward expository preaching, church discipline, and meaningful membership.

## Key Concepts
- **Nine marks** — expository preaching, biblical theology, the Gospel, conversion, evangelism, membership, discipline, discipleship, leadership
- **Church membership** — meaningful, not nominal; requires understanding of the Gospel and commitment to the body
- **Church discipline** — rarely practiced in modern evangelicalism; Dever recovered it as an act of love
- **Expository preaching** — the sermon must be governed by the biblical text

## Primary Works
- *Nine Marks of a Healthy Church* (2000)
- *The Church* (2012)
- *What Is a Healthy Church?* (2007)

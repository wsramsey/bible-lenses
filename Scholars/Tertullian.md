# Tertullian

> 🌍 Tertullian was **African** — born in Carthage (modern Tunisia), North Africa. He wrote in Latin but his African context is essential: Carthage was Rome's great rival, now a thriving intellectual city. The Latin theological vocabulary (Trinity, Person, Substance) that all Western Christianity uses is largely his invention — coined in Africa.

*155–220 · Christian · Patristic / Apologetic*

> **Theological lean:** First systematic Latin theologian, coined 'Trinity', later Montanist

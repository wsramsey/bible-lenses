# Philokalia — Orthodox Hesychast Tradition
*4th–15th century (texts) / compiled 1782 · Eastern Orthodox / Hesychasm · Transmission: written / oral / contemplative practice*

> Anthology of texts by Orthodox monks on prayer, watchfulness, and the acquisition of the Holy Spirit. Compiled by Nikodimos of the Holy Mountain and Makarios of Corinth (1782). The Jesus Prayer ('Lord Jesus Christ, Son of God, have mercy on me, a sinner') is the practice these texts describe. Transmitted through elder-disciple relationship (geronda/staret). The Way of a Pilgrim (anonymous Russian, 19th c.) popularized it. Thomas Merton, Kallistos Ware, and the modern contemplative revival all draw from it.

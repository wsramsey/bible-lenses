# African Independent Churches — Oral Theological Tradition
*19th century to present · African Christianity · Transmission: oral / musical / prophetic*

> The fastest-growing form of Christianity in the world, largely operating outside Western textual theology. Theology transmitted through prophecy, healing, song, dance, and testimony. The African Independent Churches (AICs) do theology in a fundamentally different mode — immediate experience of the Spirit, ancestor veneration questions, healing as central, the body as theological site. John Mbiti's work is the first serious academic engagement with this tradition from within.

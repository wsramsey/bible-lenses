# Brant Pitre
*1975– · Catholic · Historical Jesus Studies*

> **Why he's here:** The most academically credentialed Catholic popular biblical scholar of his generation. His *Jesus and the Jewish Roots of the Eucharist* brought serious Second Temple Jewish scholarship to a popular Catholic audience and influenced how Catholics understand the Mass. His *The Case for Jesus* is a competent popular defense of Gospel authorship.

## Key Concepts
- **Jesus in Jewish context** — serious engagement with Second Temple Judaism as the key to understanding Jesus; in the tradition of [[E.P. Sanders]] but Catholic
- **The new Exodus** — Jesus deliberately reenacts the Exodus; Passover is the key to the Last Supper
- **Manna typology** — the Eucharist as fulfillment of the manna in the wilderness; John 6 as literal
- **Messianic banquet** — the kingdom of God has a fundamentally eucharistic shape

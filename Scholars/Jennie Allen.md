# Jennie Allen
*1978– · Evangelical · Women's Discipleship*

> **Why she's here:** Founder of IF:Gathering — the most influential women's ministry platform in contemporary evangelicalism. Her *Get Out of Your Head* (2020) became a major bestseller by addressing mental health from an explicitly Christian perspective. Represents the intersection of contemporary women's ministry, discipleship, and mental health.

## Key Concepts
- **Discipleship** — the church's primary call is making disciples; IF:Gathering exists to equip women to disciple each other
- **Get out of your head** — taking captive every thought (2 Cor 10:5) as practical mental health theology
- **Community** — spiritual formation happens in relationship, not isolation

## Primary Works
- *Restless* (2013)
- *Nothing to Prove* (2017)
- *Get Out of Your Head* (2020)
- *Find Your People* (2022)

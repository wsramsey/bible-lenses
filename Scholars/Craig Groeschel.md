# Craig Groeschel
*1967– · Evangelical · Digital Ministry*

> **Why he's here:** Founder of Life.Church — one of the largest and most technologically innovative churches in America; first major multisite church to use video-teaching model. Creator of the YouVersion Bible App (500 million+ downloads). His influence is more institutional and technological than theological.

## Key Concepts
- **Church innovation** — "Do whatever it takes to reach people that nobody else is reaching"
- **Digital ministry** — YouVersion Bible App; made Scripture accessible on mobile at scale
- **Leadership** — his *Leadership Podcast* is widely used in ministry contexts

## Primary Works
- *Weird: Because Normal Isn't Working* (2011)
- *Winning the War in Your Mind* (2021)
- *Divine Direction* (2017)

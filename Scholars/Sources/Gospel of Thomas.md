*c. 50–140 AD · Gnostic / Apocryphal Gospel*

> Sayings gospel from Nag Hammadi. 114 sayings of Jesus. Some scholars see independent early tradition; others see Gnostic elaboration. Critical for understanding Jesus tradition diversity.

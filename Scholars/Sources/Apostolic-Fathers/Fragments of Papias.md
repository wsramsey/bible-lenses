# Fragments of Papias
*c. 120–130 AD · Apostolic Father*

> Fragments from Papias's lost 'Exposition of the Dominical Oracles' preserved by Eusebius and Irenaeus. Claims Matthew wrote logia in Hebrew; Mark was Peter's interpreter. Describes the Elder John as his source. Millenarian eschatology. First external testimony to Gospel authorship.

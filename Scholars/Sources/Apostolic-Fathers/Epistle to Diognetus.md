# Epistle to Diognetus
*c. 120–200 AD · Apostolic Father*

> Anonymous apology explaining Christianity to a pagan named Diognetus. Christians are in the world but not of it — 'what the soul is to the body, Christians are to the world.' Beautiful prose. Key early text on Christian identity in society. Influenced later theology of 'resident aliens' (Hauerwas/Willimon).

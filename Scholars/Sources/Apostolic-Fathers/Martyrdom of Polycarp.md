# Martyrdom of Polycarp
*c. 155–160 AD · Apostolic Father*

> Earliest detailed account of a Christian martyrdom outside the NT. Written by the Smyrnaean church as a circular letter. Polycarp refuses to deny Christ: 'Eighty-six years I have served him.' Shaped Christian theology of martyrdom, the veneration of saints, and the martyrdom date as a 'second birthday' (dies natalis). Irenaeus knew Polycarp personally.

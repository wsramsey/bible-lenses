# 2 Clement
*c. 140–160 AD · Apostolic Father*

> Earliest surviving Christian sermon (not actually a letter, despite the name). Uses Matthew, Luke, and possibly the Gospel of the Egyptians or Gospel of Thomas. Gives early evidence of NT texts being read in worship. Affirms bodily resurrection against Docetism.

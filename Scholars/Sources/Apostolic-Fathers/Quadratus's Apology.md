# Quadratus's Apology
*c. 125 AD · Apostolic Father*

> Fragment (one paragraph) preserved by Eusebius. Earliest known Christian apologetic text, presented to Emperor Hadrian. Claims people healed and raised by Jesus were still alive in his own day. First example of the apologetic genre that Justin Martyr would expand.

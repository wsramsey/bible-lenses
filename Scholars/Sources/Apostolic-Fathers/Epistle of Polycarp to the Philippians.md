# Epistle of Polycarp to the Philippians
*c. 110–140 AD · Apostolic Father*

> Letter by Bishop Polycarp of Smyrna (disciple of John the Apostle) to the church at Philippi. Quotes Paul's letters and 1 John extensively. Key evidence for early canon formation and the reverence for Paul's letters. Shows Polycarp as link between apostolic age and Irenaeus.

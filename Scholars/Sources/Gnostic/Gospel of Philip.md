# Gospel of Philip
*c. 180–250 AD · Gnostic Text*

> Valentinian Gnostic collection of sayings and reflections. Contains the famous passage about Jesus kissing Mary Magdalene (the missing word creates the Da Vinci Code speculation). Rich sacramental theology — chrism, bridal chamber. Valentinus-school. Irenaeus responds to exactly this tradition. Found at Nag Hammadi.

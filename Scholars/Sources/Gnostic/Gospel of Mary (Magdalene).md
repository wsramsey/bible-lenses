# Gospel of Mary (Magdalene)
*c. 120–180 AD · Gnostic Text*

> Mary Magdalene receives special revelation from the risen Jesus. Peter questions her authority. Gnostic: the soul's ascent past cosmic powers. Key text for feminist theology and debates about women's leadership in early Christianity. Phyllis Trible and Elisabeth Schüssler Fiorenza engage it.

# Apocryphon of John (Secret Book of John)
*c. 150–200 AD · Gnostic Text*

> The most important Sethian Gnostic text. The creation mythology: Barbelo, the fall of Sophia, the Demiurge (equated with the God of Genesis), the imprisonment of divine sparks in matter. Irenaeus's description of Gnostic myth in Against Heresies matches this text closely. Four copies found at Nag Hammadi.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apocryphon_of_John)

## Found In / Preserved In
Nag Hammadi Codices II, III, IV + Berlin Codex. Four copies — most copied Gnostic text.

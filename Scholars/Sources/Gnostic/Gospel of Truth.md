# Gospel of Truth
*c. 140–180 AD · Gnostic Text*

> Meditative homily attributed to Valentinus himself. Beautiful prose — possibly the most literary of the Gnostic texts. Error, Ignorance, and the recovery of Knowledge (gnosis) as salvation. Directly responds to the canonical Gospels and Paul. Irenaeus names Valentinus as the primary Gnostic teacher he opposes.

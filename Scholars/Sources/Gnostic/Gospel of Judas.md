# Gospel of Judas
*c. 130–180 AD · Gnostic Text*

> Sethian Gnostic text presenting Judas as the only true disciple who understood Jesus's secret teaching. Rediscovered and published 2006. Irenaeus mentions it by name (Against Heresies 1.31.1) — remarkable confirmation. Caused enormous media attention. Reframes the Judas tradition completely.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gospel_of_Judas)

## Found In / Preserved In
Codex Tchacos. Published by National Geographic 2006.

# Pistis Sophia
*c. 250–300 AD · Gnostic Text*

> Long Gnostic dialogue between the risen Jesus and his disciples (especially Mary Magdalene) over 12 years post-resurrection. Sophia's fall and redemption. Quotes the Odes of Solomon. Shows late development of Gnostic Christianity before its suppression. Important for understanding the diversity Irenaeus, Tertullian, and Origen were combating.

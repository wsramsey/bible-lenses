# Latin Vulgate
*c. 382–405 AD · Biblical Translation*

> Jerome's Latin translation commissioned by Pope Damasus. The Bible of the Western church for 1,000 years. Declared authoritative by Council of Trent. Shaped all Western theology, liturgy, and art.

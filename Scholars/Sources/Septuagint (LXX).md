# Septuagint (LXX)
*c. 280–150 BC · Biblical Translation / Source Text*

> Greek translation of Hebrew Scriptures. The Bible of the early church and NT authors. Paul and most NT writers quote LXX not Hebrew. Includes deuterocanonical books. Critical for understanding how the church read the OT.

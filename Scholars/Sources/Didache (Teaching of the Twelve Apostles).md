# Didache (Teaching of the Twelve Apostles)
*c. 50–120 AD · Early Church Document*

> Earliest surviving Christian manual of instruction. Baptism, Eucharist, fasting, church order. Discovered 1873. Shows pre-institutional Christianity. Cited by Clement of Alexandria, Origen, Eusebius.

# Nag Hammadi Library
*c. 100–400 AD (texts); discovered 1945 · Gnostic Texts*

> Collection of 52 Gnostic texts found in Egypt 1945. Gospel of Thomas, Gospel of Philip, Gospel of Truth (Valentinus). Reshaped understanding of early Christianity's diversity. Irenaeus was responding to exactly these traditions.

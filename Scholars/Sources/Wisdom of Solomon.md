# Wisdom of Solomon
*c. 100–50 BC · Deuterocanon / Apocrypha*

> Greek Jewish wisdom text. Logos theology, immortality of soul, universal scope. Major bridge between Hebrew wisdom tradition and Greek philosophy. Influenced Paul, John, Justin Martyr.

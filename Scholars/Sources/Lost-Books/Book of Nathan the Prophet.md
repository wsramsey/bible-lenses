# Book of Nathan the Prophet
*c. 10th century BC · Lost Book*

> Cited in 1 Chronicles 29:29 and 2 Chronicles 9:29. Nathan was David's court prophet. His records apparently included acts of David and Solomon. Lost. Indicates prophetic archives existed alongside court records.

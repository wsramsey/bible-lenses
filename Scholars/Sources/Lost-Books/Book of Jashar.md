# Book of Jashar
*Unknown — pre-monarchic · Lost Book*

> Cited in Joshua 10:13 (sun standing still) and 2 Samuel 1:18 (David's lament for Saul and Jonathan). Appears to be a collection of heroic poetry. Lost. Church fathers noted its existence; Luther and Calvin discussed its authority.

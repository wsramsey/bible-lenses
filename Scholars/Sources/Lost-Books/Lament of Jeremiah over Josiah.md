# Lament of Jeremiah over Josiah
*c. 609 BC · Lost Book*

> Cited in 2 Chronicles 35:25 as a written lament over King Josiah. Separate from the canonical book of Lamentations (which does not mention Josiah). A lost dirge. Evidence of a broader prophetic literary tradition.

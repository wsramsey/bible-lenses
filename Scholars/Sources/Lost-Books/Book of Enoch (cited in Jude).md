# Book of Enoch (cited in Jude)
*c. 300–100 BC · Lost Book*

> Directly quoted in Jude 14–15 ('Enoch, the seventh from Adam, prophesied...'). Also referenced in 2 Peter 2. Used by Tertullian, Origen, Irenaeus. Removed from most canons but preserved in Ethiopian Orthodox Bible. Its direct quotation in the NT makes it uniquely significant for canon debates.

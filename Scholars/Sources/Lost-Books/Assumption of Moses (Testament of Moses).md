# Assumption of Moses (Testament of Moses)
*c. 1st century AD · Lost Book*

> Possibly cited in Jude 9 (dispute over Moses's body). Partial text survives in Latin. Apocalyptic: Moses's death and heavenly assumption, prophecy of history. Early church fathers (Origen, Clement) knew it. Important for understanding Jude's use of extra-canonical tradition.

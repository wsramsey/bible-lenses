# Peshitta (Syriac Bible)
*c. 100–300 AD · Biblical Translation*

> Standard Syriac translation. Bible of the Syriac-speaking churches. Preserves early Eastern Christianity's engagement with Scripture. Different canon (omits some books in NT).

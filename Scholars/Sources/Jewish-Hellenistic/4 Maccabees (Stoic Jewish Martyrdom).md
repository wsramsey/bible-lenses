# 4 Maccabees (Stoic Jewish Martyrdom)
*c. 20–130 AD · Jewish Hellenistic*

> Philosophical treatise using the Maccabean martyrs to argue that 'devout reason is sovereign over the emotions' — a Jewish Stoicism. Written in Greek, clearly for Diaspora Jews. Influenced the theology of martyrdom, substitutionary atonement through suffering, and Christian martyrdom accounts.

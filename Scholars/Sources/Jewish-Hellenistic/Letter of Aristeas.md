# Letter of Aristeas
*c. 200–100 BC · Jewish Hellenistic*

> Account of the translation of the Torah into Greek (the Septuagint) by 72 Jewish scholars in 72 days. The name 'Septuagint' (seventy) comes from this tradition. Though legendary, establishes the LXX's divine authority for Hellenistic Judaism and the church fathers who defend using the LXX.

# Aristobulus of Alexandria (fragments)
*c. 180–145 BC · Jewish Hellenistic*

> Earliest known Jewish philosopher to systematically interpret Scripture allegorically using Greek philosophy. Claimed Plato and Aristotle derived their ideas from Moses. Fragments preserved by Eusebius and Clement of Alexandria. Precursor to Philo and the Alexandrian school — the first to say Greek philosophy depends on Torah.

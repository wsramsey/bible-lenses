# Philo of Alexandria — Complete Works
*c. 20 BC – 50 AD · Jewish Hellenistic*

> Beyond the De Opificio Mundi, Philo's complete corpus: allegorical commentaries on Genesis (Legum Allegoriae), Questions on Genesis/Exodus, the Exposition of the Law, historical works (Embassy to Gaius, Against Flaccus). The Logos theology in his works is the most direct philosophical bridge to the Gospel of John's prologue. Clement of Alexandria and Origen essentially baptize his method.

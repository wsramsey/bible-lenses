# Tobit
*c. 225–175 BC · Deuterocanon*

> Narrative of a pious Israelite in Assyrian exile. Angels, demons (Asmodeus), healing, piety, marriage theology. In Catholic/Orthodox canon. Fragment found at Qumran (4Q196-200) confirming Hebrew/Aramaic original. Influenced Raphael-archangel tradition, marriage rites, and the Tobias story in medieval art.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Book_of_Tobit)

## Found In / Preserved In
Found in Qumran Cave 4 (fragments). In LXX, Vulgate, Catholic, Orthodox Bibles.

# 1 Maccabees
*c. 100 BC · Deuterocanon*

> Historical account of the Maccabean revolt (167–134 BC) against Antiochus IV Epiphanes. The Hanukkah story. Critical background for Daniel, the Abomination of Desolation, and apocalyptic literature. Used by Josephus. Cited by Church Fathers. Essential for understanding the world Jesus entered.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1_Maccabees)

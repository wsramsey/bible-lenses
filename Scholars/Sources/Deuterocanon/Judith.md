# Judith
*c. 150 BC · Deuterocanon*

> Story of Judith who saves Israel by killing Holofernes. Historical fiction — contains anachronisms deliberately. Powerful woman as Israel's deliverer. Influenced Clement of Rome (1 Clement), Origen, Jerome (who doubted its canonicity). Major influence on medieval heroic tradition.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Book_of_Judith)

## Found In / Preserved In
LXX, Vulgate, Catholic and Orthodox Bibles. Not in Hebrew Bible or Protestant OT.

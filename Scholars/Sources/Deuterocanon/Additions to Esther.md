# Additions to Esther
*c. 100 BC · Deuterocanon*

> Six passages added to Esther in the LXX that add prayers, letters, and religious content (the Hebrew Esther famously never mentions God). In Catholic/Orthodox Bibles. Jerome separated them with notes in the Vulgate. They make Esther more explicitly theological.

# 4 Maccabees
*c. 20–130 AD · Deuterocanon*

> Philosophical treatise on the supremacy of reason over emotions using the Maccabean martyrs as examples. Written in Greek, deeply Stoic. In Eastern Orthodox canon (some). Influenced early Christian martyrdom theology and the idea of dying for truth.

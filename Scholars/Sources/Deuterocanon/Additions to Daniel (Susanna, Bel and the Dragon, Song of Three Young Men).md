# Additions to Daniel (Susanna, Bel and the Dragon, Song of Three Young Men)
*c. 167–100 BC · Deuterocanon*

> Three texts added to Daniel in the LXX: (1) Susanna — false accusation story, detective work by Daniel; (2) Bel and the Dragon — anti-idol polemic; (3) Prayer of Azariah / Song of Three Young Men — inserted into Daniel 3 (the furnace). Origen defended them against Julius Africanus who noted they weren't in Hebrew. The Susanna story influenced legal thinking.

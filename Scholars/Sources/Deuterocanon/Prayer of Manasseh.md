# Prayer of Manasseh
*c. 200–50 BC · Deuterocanon*

> Penitential prayer attributed to King Manasseh (2 Chronicles 33:13 references a prayer but doesn't include it). Beautiful prayer of confession and mercy. In Orthodox Bibles and as an appendix in some Catholic Bibles. Luther included it in his Bible as an appendix. Shaped penitential tradition.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prayer_of_Manasseh)

## Found In / Preserved In
Some LXX manuscripts, Orthodox Bibles, appendix to some Vulgate editions.

# 1 Esdras (3 Ezra)
*c. 150 BC · Deuterocanon*

> Greek version of Ezra-Nehemiah with additions including the 'Contest of the Bodyguards' story (Zerubbabel's speech on truth as the strongest thing). In LXX, some Orthodox Bibles. Origen and Eusebius cite it. Used to reconstruct chronology of the return from exile.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1_Esdras)

## Found In / Preserved In
LXX and some Orthodox Bibles. Not in Catholic Vulgate canon.

# 2 Maccabees
*c. 124 BC · Deuterocanon*

> Theological history of Maccabean revolt. Key text for bodily resurrection (2 Macc 7 — seven martyred brothers), prayers for the dead, and sin-offering for the dead (12:43-45). Directly cited by Catholics for purgatory and prayers for the dead. Luther removed it partly because of this passage. The text that created one of the Reformation's sharpest debates.

# Baruch
*c. 150–60 BC · Deuterocanon*

> Attributed to Jeremiah's scribe Baruch. Includes a Wisdom hymn (3:9–4:4) identifying Wisdom with Torah — parallel to Sirach 24. Chapter 6 is the Letter of Jeremiah. Read in Catholic/Orthodox liturgy on Holy Saturday. Cited by Athanasius, Origen, Irenaeus as 'Jeremiah.'

# Mishnah
*c. 200 AD (oral traditions older) · Rabbinic Literature*

> First major written redaction of Jewish oral law. Compiled by Rabbi Judah ha-Nasi. Foundation of Talmud. Key for understanding 1st-century Pharisaism and Paul's 'works of the law.'

# Masoretic Text (MT)
*c. 600–1000 AD (standardized) · Biblical Text*

> The authoritative Hebrew text of the Tanakh. Preserved by the Masoretes. Basis of most Protestant OT translations. Differs from LXX in key passages (Isaiah 7:14 almah vs parthenos).

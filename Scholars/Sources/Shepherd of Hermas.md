# Shepherd of Hermas
*c. 100–160 AD · Early Church Document*

> Apocalyptic text considered Scripture by some early churches (Irenaeus, Clement of Alexandria, Origen). Addressed post-baptismal sin, repentance, church discipline. Near-canonical status in some lists.

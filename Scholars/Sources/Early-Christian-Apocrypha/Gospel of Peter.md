# Gospel of Peter
*c. 100–150 AD · Early Christian Apocrypha*

> Passion narrative with unique details — the cross speaks, the risen Jesus is gigantic, the Roman soldiers witness the resurrection. First text explicitly accused of Docetism by Serapion of Antioch. Used in some Syrian churches. Eusebius mentions the controversy. Shows fluidity of Gospel tradition before canonization.

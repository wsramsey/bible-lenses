# Epistle to the Laodiceans
*c. 2nd–4th century AD · Early Christian Apocrypha*

> Short letter attributed to Paul, composed to fill the gap mentioned in Colossians 4:16 ('read the letter from Laodicea'). Jerome was skeptical but noted it was widely read. Included in many Latin manuscripts and early printed Bibles including Luther's early German Bible. Demonstrates how canonical gaps were filled.

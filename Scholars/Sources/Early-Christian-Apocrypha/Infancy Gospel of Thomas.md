# Infancy Gospel of Thomas
*c. 150 AD · Early Christian Apocrypha*

> Stories of the child Jesus performing miracles — including killing a boy who bumped him and striking teachers blind. Difficult text: the divine child as simultaneously powerful and petulant. Irenaeus knew it. Used to fill in the 'hidden years' of Jesus. Influenced Byzantine iconography of child Jesus.

# 3 Corinthians
*c. 160–200 AD · Early Christian Apocrypha*

> Exchange of letters between Paul and the Corinthian church against Gnostic teachers (Simon and Cleobius). Part of the Acts of Paul. Canonical in Armenian and Syriac churches for centuries. Defends bodily resurrection, creation by God (against Gnostic Demiurge), and prophets of Israel.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Third_Epistle_to_the_Corinthians)

## Found In / Preserved In
Canonical in Armenian Orthodox Church until recently. Part of Acts of Paul.

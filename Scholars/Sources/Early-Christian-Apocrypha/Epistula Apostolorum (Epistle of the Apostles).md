# Epistula Apostolorum (Epistle of the Apostles)
*c. 140–160 AD · Early Christian Apocrypha*

> Post-resurrection dialogue between the risen Jesus and the eleven apostles. Anti-Gnostic. Affirms bodily resurrection, future parousia, incarnation. Coptic and Ethiopic copies survive. Interesting for its anti-Docetist Christology and apocalyptic content. Represents orthodox response to Gnostic dialogues.

# Protevangelium of James
*c. 145–200 AD · Early Christian Apocrypha*

> Infancy narrative: Mary's miraculous birth, upbringing in the Temple, marriage to elderly Joseph, birth of Jesus. Source of the perpetual virginity of Mary doctrine, Joachim and Anna as Mary's parents, the ox and donkey at the manger. Origen knew it. Shaped all subsequent Catholic and Orthodox Marian theology and iconography. Hugely influential despite not being canonical.

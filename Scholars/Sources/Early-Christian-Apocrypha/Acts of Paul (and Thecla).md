# Acts of Paul (and Thecla)
*c. 160–190 AD · Early Christian Apocrypha*

> Adventures of Paul and his female disciple Thecla, who preaches and baptizes herself. Tertullian condemned it as a forgery used to defend women's right to teach and baptize. Contains the only physical description of Paul (short, bald, bandy-legged). Thecla became a major female saint — venerated in East and West.

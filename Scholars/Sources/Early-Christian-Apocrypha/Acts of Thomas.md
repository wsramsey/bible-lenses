# Acts of Thomas
*c. 200–250 AD · Early Christian Apocrypha*

> Thomas's mission to India. Contains the famous 'Hymn of the Pearl' — a Gnostic/mystical allegory of the soul's journey. Encratite (ascetic, anti-marriage). Syriac origin. Foundation legend for the Thomas Christian tradition in India (St. Thomas Christians/Nasrani) who trace their origins to apostle Thomas.

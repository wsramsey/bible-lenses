# Sirach (Ecclesiasticus / Ben Sira)
*c. 180 BC · Deuterocanon / Apocrypha*

> Hebrew wisdom text. Wisdom personified (ch. 24) as Torah. Used by Catholics, Orthodox. Shaped early Christology (Christ as Wisdom) and scholastic ethics.

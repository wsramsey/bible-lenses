# Sibylline Oracles
*c. 150 BC – 650 AD · Pseudepigrapha*

> Collection of Jewish and Christian oracles in Greek hexameter, imitating pagan Sibylline prophecy. Used by Jewish and Christian apologists to argue pagans already anticipated monotheism and the Messiah. Heavily used by Justin Martyr, Theophilus of Antioch, Clement of Alexandria, Lactantius, and Augustine.

# 2 Baruch (Syriac Apocalypse of Baruch)
*c. 100–130 AD · Pseudepigrapha*

> Jewish apocalypse parallel to 4 Ezra, responding to Jerusalem's destruction. Letter to the nine and a half tribes. Wrestles with theodicy, Torah as Israel's permanent possession, resurrection. Written after 70 AD, companion text to 4 Ezra. Shapes understanding of Jewish-Christian divergence after 70 AD.

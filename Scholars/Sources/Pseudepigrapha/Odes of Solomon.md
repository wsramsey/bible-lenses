# Odes of Solomon
*c. 100 AD · Pseudepigrapha*

> Earliest surviving Christian hymn collection (42 odes). Probably from Syria. Deep Johannine and wisdom themes. May be among earliest Christian liturgical poetry. Cited by Lactantius. Rediscovered in 1909. Illuminates early Christian worship and mystical union language.

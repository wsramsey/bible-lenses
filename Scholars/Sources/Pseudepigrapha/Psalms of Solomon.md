# Psalms of Solomon
*c. 70–40 BC · Pseudepigrapha*

> 18 psalms responding to Pompey's invasion of Jerusalem (63 BC). Anticipate a Davidic Messiah who will purge Israel of sinners and Gentiles. Key text for 1st-century Jewish Messianic expectation — exactly the kind of Messiah Jesus was NOT according to NT. Referenced by Origen, in some LXX manuscripts.

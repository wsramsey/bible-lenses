# Life of Adam and Eve (Apocalypse of Moses)
*c. 100 BC – 200 AD · Pseudepigrapha*

> Narrative expanding Genesis 3 — Adam and Eve's life after the expulsion. Includes Satan's fall story (his refusal to worship Adam), death of Adam, Eve's account of the Fall. Influenced Milton's Paradise Lost, medieval iconography of the Fall, and patristic reflection on original sin.

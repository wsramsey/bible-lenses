# 2 Enoch (Slavonic Enoch / Book of the Secrets of Enoch)
*c. 1st century AD · Pseudepigrapha*

> Enoch's heavenly journey through the ten heavens. More elaborate than 1 Enoch. Preserved only in Slavonic. The ten-heaven cosmology influenced later mysticism, Dante, and the Merkabah tradition. Key for understanding NT heavenly journey texts (2 Cor 12, Revelation).

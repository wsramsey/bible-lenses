# 4 Ezra (2 Esdras 3-14)
*c. 100 AD · Pseudepigrapha*

> Jewish apocalypse responding to destruction of Jerusalem (70 AD), written as if from Ezra after 586 BC. Profound wrestling with theodicy — why did God allow this? Strong predestination, few will be saved, fiery punishment. Influenced Revelation, 2 Baruch, Augustine on predestination. Jerome added it as an appendix to the Vulgate.

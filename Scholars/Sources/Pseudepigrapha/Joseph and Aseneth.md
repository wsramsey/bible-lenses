# Joseph and Aseneth
*c. 100 BC – 200 AD · Pseudepigrapha*

> Romantic expansion of Genesis 41:45 — how could the patriarch Joseph marry an Egyptian? Aseneth converts. Key text for understanding Jewish views on Gentile conversion, proselytism, and possibly early Christian baptismal practice (honeycomb vision). Cited by church fathers.

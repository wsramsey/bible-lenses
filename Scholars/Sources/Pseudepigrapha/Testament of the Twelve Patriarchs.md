# Testament of the Twelve Patriarchs
*c. 200 BC – 200 AD (complex redaction) · Pseudepigrapha*

> Deathbed speeches of Jacob's twelve sons. Contains ethical teaching, prophecy, and Messianic expectation. Fragments found at Qumran (Testament of Levi, Naphtali). Christian interpolations throughout. Direct influence on Paul's virtue/vice lists (Gal 5) and NT ethical paraenesis.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Testaments_of_the_Twelve_Patriarchs)

# Ascension of Isaiah
*c. 100–200 AD · Pseudepigrapha*

> Two-part text: Martyrdom of Isaiah (death by sawing) + Vision of Isaiah (heavenly journey). The Martyrdom likely Jewish; the Vision is Christian. Christ descends incognito through the seven heavens at the Incarnation. Cited by Origen, possibly referenced in Hebrews 11:37 ('sawn in two'). Key for early Christology.

# War Scroll (1QM — Milhamah)
*c. 100–50 BC · Dead Sea Scroll*

> Apocalyptic battle plan for the final 40-year war between Sons of Light (Israel) and Sons of Darkness (Gentiles/evil). Detailed military organization, liturgy, trumpet calls, battle standards. Directly illuminates the 'spiritual warfare' language in Paul (Eph 6) and the cosmic dualism of John's Gospel.

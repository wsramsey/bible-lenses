# Melchizedek Scroll (11QMelch)
*c. 100–75 BC · Dead Sea Scroll*

> Eschatological midrash on Melchizedek as a heavenly figure who will execute judgment on Belial. Connects Psalm 82, Isaiah 61, and Leviticus 25 (Jubilee). Crucial background for the Epistle to the Hebrews (Melchizedek Christology, chapters 5–7) and the angelic Christology debate in early Christianity.

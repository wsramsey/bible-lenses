# Songs of the Sabbath Sacrifice (4QShirShab)
*c. 100–50 BC · Dead Sea Scroll*

> 13 angelic Sabbath liturgies. Describes heavenly temple worship, angelic priests, divine throne-chariot (Merkabah). Key background for Hebrews (heavenly tabernacle), Revelation's heavenly throne room, Paul's 'third heaven,' and early Jewish mysticism that influenced Christian contemplative tradition.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Songs_of_the_Sabbath_Sacrifice)

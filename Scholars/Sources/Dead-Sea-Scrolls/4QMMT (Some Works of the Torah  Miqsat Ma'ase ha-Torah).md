# 4QMMT (Some Works of the Torah / Miqsat Ma'ase ha-Torah)
*c. 150 BC · Dead Sea Scroll*

> Halakhic letter from Qumran leadership to Jerusalem priestly authorities, listing 22 disputed legal points. Contains the phrase 'works of the Torah' (ma'ase ha-Torah) — the exact phrase Paul uses in Galatians and Romans. E.P. Sanders and James Dunn argue this revolutionizes understanding of 'works of the law' in Paul — they are boundary markers, not merit-earning.

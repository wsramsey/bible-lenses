# Thanksgiving Hymns (Hodayot / 1QH)
*c. 150–50 BC · Dead Sea Scroll*

> Collection of 30+ psalms of thanksgiving, possibly authored by the Teacher of Righteousness. Personal, intense, first-person spirituality. Themes: humility, election, divine grace, praise amidst suffering. Remarkable parallels with Paul's language of weakness, grace, and the elect community. Shaped understanding of Jewish personal piety.

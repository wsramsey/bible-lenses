# Genesis Apocryphon (1QapGen)
*c. 50 BC – 50 AD · Dead Sea Scroll*

> Aramaic expansion of Genesis — especially Lamech, Noah, and Abraham narratives. Part of a broader tradition of rewriting Scripture (Jubilees, later Targums). Shows how Scripture was being read and expanded in Jesus's time. Direct comparison with NT parables and Matthew's genealogy.

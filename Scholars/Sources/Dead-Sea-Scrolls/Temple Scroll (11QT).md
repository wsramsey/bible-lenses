# Temple Scroll (11QT)
*c. 150–125 BC · Dead Sea Scroll*

> Longest of the Dead Sea Scrolls (8.1m). An alternative Torah written in first person as if God speaking directly to Moses. Describes an ideal Temple, purity laws, festival calendar. Rewrites Deuteronomy. Key for understanding Jesus's Temple action, Jewish halakha debates, and the background of the Epistle to the Hebrews.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Temple_Scroll)

## Found In / Preserved In
Cave 11, Qumran. Purchased by Yigael Yadin from Kando in 1967.

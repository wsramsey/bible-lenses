# Great Isaiah Scroll (1QIsa-a)
*c. 125 BC · Dead Sea Scroll*

> The oldest complete manuscript of any biblical book. Contains all 66 chapters of Isaiah. Predates the Masoretic Text by 1,000 years. Confirms essential stability of the Hebrew text while showing variant readings. Key for Isaiah 53 ('Suffering Servant') scholarship — the text most cited in NT about Jesus.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Isaiah_scroll)

## Found In / Preserved In
Cave 1, Qumran. Now in Shrine of the Book, Jerusalem.

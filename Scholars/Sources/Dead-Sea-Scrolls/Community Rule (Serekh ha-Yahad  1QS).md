# Community Rule (Serekh ha-Yahad / 1QS)
*c. 100–50 BC · Dead Sea Scroll*

> The foundational governing document of the Qumran community. Describes entrance rites (baptism-like immersions), communal meals, the Two Spirits (light/darkness), organizational structure. Parallels to NT: John the Baptist's community, the Last Supper, early church community. The phrase 'way in the wilderness' (Isa 40:3) is used here exactly as in Mark 1:3.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Community_Rule)

## Found In / Preserved In
Cave 1, Qumran. Multiple copies found.

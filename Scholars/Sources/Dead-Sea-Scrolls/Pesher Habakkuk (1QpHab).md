# Pesher Habakkuk (1QpHab)
*c. 50–25 BC · Dead Sea Scroll*

> Verse-by-verse commentary on Habakkuk 1–2, applying the text to the Qumran community's own history. The 'Teacher of Righteousness' vs the 'Wicked Priest.' Demonstrates the pesher method of interpretation — the text's true meaning is hidden and now revealed in present events. The exact same hermeneutic used in Matthew's fulfillment formulas ('This was to fulfill what was written...').

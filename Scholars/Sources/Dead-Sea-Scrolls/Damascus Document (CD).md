# Damascus Document (CD)
*c. 100 BC (copy found in Cairo Geniza 1896) · Dead Sea Scroll*

> Rules for Qumran communities living outside the main settlement (in 'camps'). Admonitions, community laws, calendar. Unique because it was known from a medieval Cairo Geniza copy before Qumran discovery confirmed it. Contains the 'star out of Jacob' Messianic interpretation.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Damascus_document)

Cairo Geniza (1896) + Cave 4 & 6 fragments

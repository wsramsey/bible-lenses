# Marcion's Canon
*c. 140 AD · Rejected Canon*

> Marcion rejected the OT entirely and kept only edited Luke + 10 Pauline letters. Forced the Church to define its own canon. Irenaeus, Tertullian, and Origen all respond directly to Marcion. The first 'canon' provoked the Catholic response.

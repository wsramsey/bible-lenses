# Paul Tillich
*1886–1965 · Christian · Liberal Protestant / Existentialist*

> **Theological lean:** God as Ground of Being, Correlation method, Symbol, Ultimate concern

## Hermeneutical Lenses

- [[Lens - Existentialist]] — [Existentialist](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Christian_existentialism)
  *Meaning through personal authentic encounter; Bultmann, Kierkegaard, Tillich*
- [[Lens - Phenomenological]] — [Phenomenological](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phenomenology_of_religion)
  *Experience of the sacred as primary; Ricoeur, Otto, Marion*
- [[Lens - Philosophical Theology]] — [Philosophical Theology](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philosophical_theology)
  *Applies philosophy to theological questions; Aquinas, Tillich, Plantinga*

## Shaped By

- [[Martin Buber]] — *philosophical*

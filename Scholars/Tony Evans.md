# Tony Evans
*1949– · Evangelical · Kingdom Theology*

> **Why he's here:** The most influential African-American evangelical pastor-theologian of his generation. Founder of Oak Cliff Bible Fellowship (Dallas) and the Urban Alternative. The first African American to earn a doctorate from Dallas Theological Seminary. His "kingdom agenda" theology — that the kingdom of God is the unifying framework for all of Christian life — has shaped urban evangelical ministry across racial lines.

## Key Concepts
- **Kingdom agenda** — God's kingdom is the comprehensive framework for individual, family, church, and community life; not just personal salvation
- **Urban shalom** — the church's responsibility to transform neighborhoods, not just save individuals
- **Racial reconciliation** — a consistent voice for racial unity within evangelical Christianity
- **Expository preaching** — verse-by-verse exposition as the primary pastoral method

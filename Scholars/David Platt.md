# David Platt
*1979– · Evangelical · Radical Discipleship*

> **Why he's here:** His book *Radical* (2010) became the evangelical manifesto for a generation reacting against the prosperity gospel and comfortable American Christianity. Former president of the IMB (International Mission Board). His preaching style — hour-plus expository sermons with global mission application — defined a strand of serious young evangelical culture.

## Key Concepts
- **Radical** — the American Dream is antithetical to the Gospel; disciples of Jesus are called to sacrifice comfort for the nations
- **Unreached peoples** — the primary mission of the church is to take the Gospel to peoples who have never heard it
- **Counter-cultural discipleship** — downward mobility, simplicity, risk

## Primary Works
- *Radical* (2010)
- *Follow Me* (2013)
- *Counter Culture* (2015)

# Russell Moore
*1971– · Evangelical · Kingdom Ethics*

> **Why he's here:** Former president of the SBC's Ethics and Religious Liberty Commission (2013–2021), now editor-in-chief of *Christianity Today*. Moore became a significant voice of evangelical dissent from Trumpism and the SBC's sex abuse cover-up crisis. His *Onward* (2015) articulated a "kingdom first" alternative to culture war Christianity.

## Key Concepts
- **Exile ecclesiology** — American Christians are now cultural exiles; this is a gift, not a crisis; the church can be the church
- **Kingdom ethics** — the church witnesses to the kingdom by living kingdom values now, not by winning political power
- **Racial reconciliation** — consistent advocate within the SBC for racial justice
- **Institutional critique** — his 2021 resignation letter to the SBC revealed systematic sex abuse cover-up

## Primary Works
- *Onward: Engaging the Culture without Losing the Gospel* (2015)
- *Tempted and Tried* (2011)
- *The Storm-Tossed Family* (2018)

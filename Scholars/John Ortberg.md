# John Ortberg
*1957– · Evangelical · Spiritual Formation*

> **Why he's here:** The most effective popularizer of [[Dallas Willard]]'s spiritual formation theology. Ortberg was mentored directly by Willard and translated his dense philosophical categories into accessible pastoral writing. His *The Life You've Always Wanted* is the standard entry point to Willard's world for ordinary churchgoers.

## Key Concepts
- **Soul training** — spiritual disciplines as training (not just trying); the body and habits are the site of transformation
- **With God** — the fundamental Christian reality is not doing things for God but being with God
- **Spiritual formation** — the intentional, sustained process of being transformed into Christlikeness
- **Risk** — his *If You Want to Walk on Water, You've Got to Get Out of the Boat* emphasizes faith as venture

## Shaped By
- [[Dallas Willard]] (direct mentorship; decisive influence)
- [[Richard Foster]]
- Willow Creek Community Church (staff there under Bill Hybels)

## Primary Works
- *The Life You've Always Wanted* (1997)
- *If You Want to Walk on Water* (2001)
- *Soul Keeping* (2014, on Willard)
- *Eternity Is Now in Session* (2018)

# Yves Congar
*1904–1995 · Catholic · Ecclesiology & Ecumenism*

> **Why he's here:** The architect of Vatican II ecclesiology. Congar spent decades under Roman suspicion (forbidden to teach, publish, or lecture at various times), then watched his ideas become official Catholic teaching at Vatican II. His three-volume *I Believe in the Holy Spirit* is the most important Catholic pneumatology of the 20th century.

## Key Concepts
- **Tradition and traditions** — living Tradition as the Spirit-guided transmission of revelation; distinct from mere human traditions
- **Laity** — the whole people of God share in Christ's priestly, prophetic, and kingly offices; not passive recipients
- **Ecumenism** — genuine engagement with Protestant and Orthodox theology as necessary for Catholic self-understanding
- **Pneumatology** — recovery of the Holy Spirit as co-constitutive of the church, not just the papacy's guarantee

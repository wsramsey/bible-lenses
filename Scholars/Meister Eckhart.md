# Meister Eckhart
*1260–1328 · Christian · Rhineland Mysticism / Dominican*

> **Theological lean:** Union with Godhead, Gelassenheit, Apophatic beyond Trinity

## Hermeneutical Lenses

- [[Lens - Mystical / Apophatic]] — [Mystical / Apophatic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apophatic_theology)
  *God beyond language; silence, negation, union; Gregory of Nyssa, Eckhart*
- [[Lens - Allegorical / Spiritual]] — [Allegorical / Spiritual](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Allegorical_interpretation_of_the_Bible)
  *Text has deeper spiritual meaning beyond literal; Origen, Eckhart*
- [[Lens - Phenomenological]] — [Phenomenological](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phenomenology_of_religion)
  *Experience of the sacred as primary; Ricoeur, Otto, Marion*

## Shaped By

- [[Pseudo-Dionysius the Areopagite]] — *mystical*
  > Eckhart's apophatic theology directly inherits the Dionysian tradition
- [[Thomas Aquinas]] — *theological*
  > Eckhart was a Dominican; engages Thomism even while radicalizing it

## Influenced

- [[Thomas Merton]] — *direct*
  > Merton wrote extensively on Eckhart; contemplative nondualism

# Kevin DeYoung
*1977– · Evangelical · Reformed Pastor-Theologian*

> **Why he's here:** The most readable and prolific young Reformed pastor-theologian of his generation. His books on busyness, holiness, and the Christian life are widely used in conservative evangelical churches. Senior pastor of Christ Covenant Church (Matthews, NC) and professor at Reformed Theological Seminary.

## Key Concepts
- **Ordinary faithfulness** — holiness is not dramatic but daily; small obediences compounded over time
- **The hole in our holiness** — evangelical culture talks about grace but neglects the call to actual holy living
- **Reformed confessionalism** — the Westminster Confession and Reformed tradition as the best summary of biblical teaching

## Primary Works
- *Just Do Something* (2009)
- *The Hole in Our Holiness* (2012)
- *Crazy Busy* (2013)
- *The Biggest Story* (2015)

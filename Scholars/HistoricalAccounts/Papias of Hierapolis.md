# Papias of Hierapolis
*60–130 AD · Apostolic Father*

> Bishop of Hierapolis, disciple of the Apostle John. Fragments preserved by Eusebius. Claims Matthew wrote logia in Hebrew; Mark was Peter's interpreter. First witness to Gospel authorship traditions. Millenarian — influenced later premillennialism.

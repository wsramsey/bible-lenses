# Suetonius
*69–122 AD · Roman Historian*

> Life of Claudius mentions expulsion of Jews from Rome 'at the instigation of Chrestus' (c. 49 AD) — possibly referring to disputes about Christ. Corroborates Acts 18:2 (Aquila and Priscilla).

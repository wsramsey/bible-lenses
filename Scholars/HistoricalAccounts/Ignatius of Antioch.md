# Ignatius of Antioch
*35–108 AD · Apostolic Father / Martyr*

> Bishop of Antioch, likely knew the Apostles. Letters written en route to martyrdom in Rome. First use of 'Catholic Church.' Strongly episcopal polity, Eucharist as medicine of immortality, anti-Docetism. Formative for church order and incarnational theology.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ignatius_of_Antioch)

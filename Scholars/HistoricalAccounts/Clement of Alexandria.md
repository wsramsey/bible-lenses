# Clement of Alexandria
*150–215 AD · Church Father / Theologian*

> Head of Catechetical School of Alexandria before Origen. First to systematically integrate Greek philosophy (especially Plato, Stoics) with Christian theology. Christian 'Gnostic' (true knowledge). Teacher of Origen. Logos as divine teacher of all humanity.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Clement_of_Alexandria)

## Works
- *Protrepticus*
- *Paedagogus*
- *Stromata*
- *Who Is the Rich Man That Shall Be Saved?*

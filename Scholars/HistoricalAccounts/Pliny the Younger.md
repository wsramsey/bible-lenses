# Pliny the Younger
*61–113 AD · Roman Governor / Writer*

> Roman governor of Bithynia. Letter to Emperor Trajan (c. 112 AD) describes Christians gathering before dawn to sing hymns to Christ 'as to a god.' Earliest pagan account of Christian worship practice.

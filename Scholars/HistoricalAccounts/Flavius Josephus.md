# Flavius Josephus
*37–100 AD · Jewish Historian*

> Jewish-Roman historian. Antiquities includes the Testimonium Flavianum (disputed reference to Jesus) and James the brother of Jesus. Jewish War describes the destruction of the Temple (70 AD). Essential background for NT and 1st-century Judaism.

# Eusebius of Caesarea
*260–340 AD · Church Historian*

> Father of Church History. Ecclesiastical History traces Christianity from Christ to Constantine. Preserves otherwise lost fragments of 2nd-3rd century writers. First to articulate state-church alliance theology. Shaped how all subsequent history reads the early church.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eusebius)

# Polycarp of Smyrna
*69–155 AD · Apostolic Father / Martyr*

> Bishop of Smyrna, disciple of the Apostle John. Direct link between the Apostolic age and the Ante-Nicene Fathers. His martyrdom account is the earliest outside the NT. Key figure in transmission of apostolic tradition to Irenaeus.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Polycarp)

## Works
- *Epistle to the Philippians*
- *Martyrdom of Polycarp*

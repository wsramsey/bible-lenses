# Maximus the Confessor
*580–662 AD · Eastern Church Father / Mystic*

> Greatest Byzantine theologian. Synthesized Origen, Pseudo-Dionysius, and Cappadocians. Cosmic Christology — the Logos as the principle (logos) behind every created thing. Defender of Chalcedonian two-wills doctrine against Monothelitism. Torture and exile for refusing compromise.

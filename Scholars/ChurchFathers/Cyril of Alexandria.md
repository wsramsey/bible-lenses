# Cyril of Alexandria
*376–444 AD · Alexandrian / Pre-Chalcedonian*

> Archbishop of Alexandria. Champion of Theotokos (Mary as God-bearer) against Nestorius. His Christology won at the Council of Ephesus (431). Shaped Eastern Orthodox Christology. Complex figure — also responsible for violence against pagans and Jews in Alexandria.

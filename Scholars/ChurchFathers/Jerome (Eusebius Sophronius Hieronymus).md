# Jerome (Eusebius Sophronius Hieronymus)
*347–420 AD · Latin Church Father*

> Translator of the Latin Vulgate Bible — the definitive Western Bible for 1,000 years. Learned Hebrew from Jewish teachers. Ascetic hermit. Fierce controversialist against Origen's speculations. His canon decisions (protocanonical vs apocryphal) shaped Protestant biblical canon.

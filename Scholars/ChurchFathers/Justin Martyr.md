# Justin Martyr
*100–165 AD · Pre-Nicene / Apologetics*

> First great Christian apologist. Logos theology — Christ as the pre-existent Logos present in all truth (including Plato). Laid foundation for Christian engagement with Greek philosophy. Justin shows Christianity is the 'true philosophy.'

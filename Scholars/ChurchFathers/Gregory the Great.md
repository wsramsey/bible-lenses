# Gregory the Great
*540–604 AD · Latin Church Father / Pope*

> Pope who shaped medieval Western Christianity more than any other. Pastoral Rule — the handbook of Christian ministry for 1,000 years. Defined purgatory, developed Gregorian chant tradition, sent Augustine of Canterbury to England. Augustinian in theology but practical in orientation.

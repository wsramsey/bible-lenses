# Gregory of Nazianzus
*329–390 AD · Cappadocian Father*

> The Theologian. His Five Theological Orations at Constantinople (380) became definitive Trinitarian theology — what became Nicene orthodoxy. Formulated the Spirit's full divinity most clearly. Deeply poetic theologian. Friends with Basil of Caesarea.

# Cyprian of Carthage
*200–258 AD · North African Church Father*

> Bishop of Carthage and martyr. 'Outside the church there is no salvation' — first clear formulation. Defined ecclesiology around bishop's authority. Major role in schism/restoration debates. Augustine builds on his ecclesiology.

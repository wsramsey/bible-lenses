# John of Damascus
*675–749 AD · Eastern Church Father*

> Last of the Greek Fathers. His Fount of Knowledge (Exact Exposition) is the first systematic theology of the Eastern church — organized all previous patristic theology. Defended icons against Iconoclasm. His systematic method influenced Aquinas's Summa structure.

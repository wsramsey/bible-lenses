# Lactantius
*250–325 AD · Latin Apologist*

> Called the Christian Cicero. First Latin systematic theology (Divine Institutes). Apologetics for educated Romans. Constantine's sons tutor. Preserves information about Diocletianic persecution. Bridge between classical rhetoric and Christian theology.

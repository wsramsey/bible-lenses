# Leo the Great
*400–461 AD · Latin Church Father / Pope*

> Pope who defined the two-natures Christology at Chalcedon (451) via his Tome. Expanded papal authority — met Attila the Hun. The Tome of Leo is the Chalcedonian definition's backbone and divides Catholic/Orthodox from Oriental Orthodox to this day.

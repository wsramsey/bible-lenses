# Bernard of Clairvaux
*1090–1153 AD · Cistercian / Medieval Mystic*

> Most influential churchman of the 12th century. Mystical theology of love — union with God through love not intellect. Launched Second Crusade. Opposed Abelard. His sermons on Song of Songs are the foundation of medieval bridal mysticism. Influenced Luther, Calvin, and modern devotional tradition.

# Ambrose of Milan
*340–397 AD · Latin Church Father*

> Bishop of Milan, mentor of Augustine. His sermons and allegorical interpretation of Scripture triggered Augustine's intellectual conversion to Christianity. Introduced Eastern hymnody to the West. Key figure in church-state relations (confronted Emperor Theodosius).

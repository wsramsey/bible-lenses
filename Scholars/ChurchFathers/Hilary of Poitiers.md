# Hilary of Poitiers
*310–367 AD · Latin Church Father*

> Called the Athanasius of the West. Defender of Nicene orthodoxy against Arianism in Latin church. Introduced Eastern Trinitarian theology to the West. Influenced Augustine, Jerome. First Latin poet of the church.

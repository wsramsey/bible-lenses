# Max Lucado
*1955– · Evangelical · Popular Pastoral*

> **Why he's here:** The best-selling Christian author in America — over 130 million books sold. His gift is making the grace and love of God emotionally accessible to ordinary people who don't read theology. Not a theologian but a pastor-storyteller whose reach dwarfs most serious theologians.

## Key Concepts
- **You Are Special** — his defining metaphor; God's unconditional love for each person as the foundation of identity
- **Grace** — central theme across all works; God's unearned favor as the counter to performance religion
- **Anxiety** — his *Anxious for Nothing* (on Phil 4) became a best-seller during COVID

## Primary Works
- *In the Grip of Grace* (1996)
- *He Chose the Nails* (2000)
- *Anxious for Nothing* (2017)
- Numerous children's books

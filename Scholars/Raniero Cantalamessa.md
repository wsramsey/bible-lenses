# Raniero Cantalamessa
*1934– · Catholic · Charismatic Renewal*

> **Why he's here:** The Preacher to the Papal Household since 1980 — he delivers Advent and Lent sermons to the Pope and the Roman Curia. A patristics scholar and leader in the Catholic Charismatic Renewal, Cantalamessa bridges rigorous patristic scholarship and experiential charismatic spirituality in a way few others manage.

## Key Concepts
- **The Passover of the Lord** — his central work; the Paschal mystery as the key to all Christian theology and liturgy, traced from Exodus through the Fathers
- **The Holy Spirit in the life of Jesus** — pneumatological Christology; Jesus lived his life in the power of the Spirit as the model for Christians
- **Unction of the Spirit** — the anointing (chrism) as the fundamental charismatic grace underlying all ministries
- **Patristic ecumenism** — the Fathers belong to all Christians; uses patristic sources to bridge Catholic-Protestant-Orthodox divides

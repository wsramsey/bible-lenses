# Tim Keller
*1950–2023 · Christian · Reformed / Evangelical / Urban*

> **Theological lean:** Reformed apologetics, Cultural engagement, Prodigal God, Center Church

## Hermeneutical Lenses

- [[Lens - Redemptive-Historical]] — [Redemptive-Historical](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Redemptive-historical_hermeneutics)
  *Reads OT as foreshadowing fulfilled in Christ; Vos, Ridderbos*
- [[Lens - Apologetic]] — [Apologetic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Christian_apologetics)
  *Defense of Christian faith against objections; Lewis, Schaeffer, McGrath*
- [[Lens - Narrative / Literary]] — [Narrative / Literary](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Narrative_theology)
  *Scripture as story; literary analysis of plot, character; Alter, Frei*

## Shaped By

- [[Francis Schaeffer]] — *direct*
  > Keller read Schaeffer at InterVarsity; shaped his apologetic instinct
- [[C.S. Lewis]] — *direct*
  > Lewis's imaginative apologetics deeply shapes Keller's method
- [[Jonathan Edwards]] — *direct*
  > Keller's theology of joy and beauty is consciously Edwardsean
- [[Richard Baxter]] — *formative*
  > Keller's pastoral theology draws on Baxter's Reformed Pastor model

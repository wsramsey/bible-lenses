# Andy Crouch
*1968– · Evangelical · Cultural Theology*

> **Why he's here:** The most thoughtful evangelical voice on culture, technology, and power. His *Culture Making* (2008) moved beyond the "Christ against culture / Christ transforming culture" binary to ask: what are Christians creating? His *The Tech-Wise Family* and *The Life We're Looking For* are the most theologically grounded treatments of technology and personhood in contemporary evangelical literature.

## Key Concepts
- **Culture making** — Christians are called not just to consume or critique culture but to make it; creation as the paradigm
- **Power and vulnerability** — genuine power requires genuine vulnerability; institutions become corrupt when they eliminate vulnerability
- **Devices and persons** — technology promises power without relationship; the body and embodied community are the antidote
- **Strong and weak** — the cross reveals that God's power operates through weakness; institutions that protect only the strong have lost the Gospel

# Jürgen Moltmann
*1926–2024 · Christian · Progressive Reformed / Hope Theology*

> **Theological lean:** Theology of Hope, Crucified God, Social Trinity, Political theology

## Hermeneutical Lenses

- [[Lens - Narrative / Literary]] — [Narrative / Literary](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Narrative_theology)
  *Scripture as story; literary analysis of plot, character; Alter, Frei*
- [[Lens - Liberation Hermeneutics]] — [Liberation Hermeneutics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Liberation_theology)
  *Reads from underside of history; poor, marginalized; Gutiérrez, Cone*
- [[Lens - Philosophical Theology]] — [Philosophical Theology](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philosophical_theology)
  *Applies philosophy to theological questions; Aquinas, Tillich, Plantinga*

## Shaped By

- [[Karl Barth]] — *foundational*
  > Moltmann inherits Barth's dialectical method while critiquing his impassibility
- [[Søren Kierkegaard]] — *formative*
  > Moltmann engages Kierkegaard's concept of despair in Theology of Hope

## Influenced

- [[Miroslav Volf]] — *direct*
  > Volf studied under Moltmann; social Trinity and embrace theology

## Primary Works

- *Theology of Hope*
- *The Crucified God*
- *The Trinity and the Kingdom*

# Athanasius of Alexandria

> 🌍 Athanasius was **African** — Bishop of Alexandria, Egypt. He was likely of Coptic (native Egyptian) descent, not Greek. The man who defined Trinitarian orthodoxy and successfully defended it against the entire Roman imperial establishment ('Athanasius contra mundum' — Athanasius against the world) was an African bishop. The Nicene Creed is largely his achievement.

*296–373 · Christian · Patristic / Nicene Orthodox*

> **Theological lean:** Homoousios, Incarnation as deification

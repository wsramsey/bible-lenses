# Plato

> Forms, soul, immortality, the Good — foundation of Western philosophical theology

## Influenced

- [[Philo of Alexandria]] — *philosophical* · Platonic Forms and Logos applied to Jewish Scripture
- [[Origen of Alexandria]] — *philosophical* · Platonic pre-existence of souls, allegory, ascent of the soul
- [[Pseudo-Dionysius the Areopagite]] — *philosophical* · Proclus and Neoplatonism deeply shape the Areopagite's hierarchy and apophasis
- [[Gregory of Nyssa]] — *philosophical* · Platonic soul-ascent reframed as infinite epektasis toward God
- [[Paul Ricoeur]] — *philosophical* · Ricoeur's philosophy of will and imagination draws on Platonic sources
- [[Rudolf Otto]] — *philosophical* · Platonic sense of the transcendent beauty shapes Otto's numinous
- [[Philo of Alexandria]] — *philosophical* · Philo fuses Platonic philosophy with Jewish Scripture
- [[Maimonides Rambam]] — *philosophical* · Maimonides mediated Aristotelianism into Jewish thought as Aquinas did for Christianity
- [[John D. Caputo]] — *philosophical* · Caputo deconstructs Platonic metaphysics through Derrida to get to a 'weak' theology

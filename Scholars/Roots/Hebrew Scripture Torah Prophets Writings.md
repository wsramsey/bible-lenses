# Hebrew Scripture (Torah, Prophets, Writings)

> The Tanakh — foundational source of all Jewish and Christian thought

## Influenced

- [[Philo of Alexandria]] — *primary-source* · Philo interprets Torah through Platonic allegory
- [[Paul of Tarsus]] — *primary-source* · Paul reads Torah as fulfilled and transformed in Christ
- [[Rashi Rabbi Shlomo Yitzchaki]] — *primary-source* · Rashi's entire work is peshat commentary on Torah and Talmud
- [[Abraham Joshua Heschel]] — *primary-source* · Heschel's entire thought is a retrieval of biblical prophetic consciousness

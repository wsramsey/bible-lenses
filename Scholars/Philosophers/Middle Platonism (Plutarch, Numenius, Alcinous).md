# Middle Platonism (Plutarch, Numenius, Alcinous)
*c. 80 BC – 220 AD · Middle Platonism*

> Bridge between Plato and Plotinus/Neoplatonism. Developed transcendent First God, Logos/Demiurge, World Soul. Directly shaped Philo, Clement of Alexandria, Origen, and early Christian theology. Numenius: 'What is Plato but Moses speaking Greek?'

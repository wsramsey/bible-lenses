# Cicero
*106–43 BC · Roman Stoicism / Rhetoric*

> Roman orator who transmitted Greek philosophy (Stoicism, Platonism) to the Latin West. Natural law, duties, rhetoric, republic. Shaped Augustine's conversion trajectory (Hortensius), Lactantius's style, Ambrose's On Duties, and medieval political theology.

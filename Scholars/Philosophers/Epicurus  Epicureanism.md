# Epicurus / Epicureanism
*341–270 BC · Epicurean*

> Materialism, atoms, pleasure as absence of pain, no divine providence, no afterlife. The main philosophical opponent early Christian apologists (Paul at Athens, Justin, Tertullian, Origen) argued against.

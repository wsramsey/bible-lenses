# Pythagoras
*570–495 BC · Pre-Socratic / Pythagorean*

> Mathematics as divine reality, immortality of the soul, reincarnation. Influenced Plato's soul theory. Pythagorean number mysticism influenced Jewish and Christian numerology (gematria, number theology in Revelation).

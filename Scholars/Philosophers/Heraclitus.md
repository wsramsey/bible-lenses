# Heraclitus
*c. 535–475 BC · Pre-Socratic*

> Pre-Socratic philosopher who introduced the concept of Logos as the rational principle governing the universe. His Logos concept was the seed from which Stoic Logos theology, Philo's Logos, and ultimately John 1:1 ('In the beginning was the Logos') grew.

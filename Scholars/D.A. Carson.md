# D.A. Carson
*1946– · Evangelical · Biblical Theology*

> **Why he's here:** The most rigorous and prolific evangelical New Testament scholar of his generation. Carson co-founded The Gospel Coalition, wrote the standard evangelical commentary on John, and has been the most consistent voice for a robustly theological and hermeneutically sophisticated evangelicalism against both liberal drift and populist shallowness.

## Key Concepts
- **Sola Scriptura** — Scripture as the final authority; but not *nuda* Scriptura; tradition and community matter in interpretation
- **Biblical theology** — the unfolding story of redemption across the canon; Scripture interprets Scripture through its own narrative
- **The Gagging of God** — his major apologetic work; pluralism and postmodernism threaten the particularity of the Gospel
- **Hermeneutics** — his *Exegetical Fallacies* is required reading for NT interpretation; common mistakes dissected

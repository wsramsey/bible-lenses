# Council of Jerusalem
*c. 49 AD · Apostolic Council*

> Acts 15. First Christian council. Resolved whether Gentile converts must keep Torah. Decision: Gentiles need not be circumcised — opened Christianity beyond Judaism. Foundation for Paul's Gentile mission theology.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Council_of_Jerusalem)

## Doctrine Resolved
**Gentiles and Torah observance**

## Key Participants
- Paul of Tarsus
- James the Just
- Peter

# Westminster Assembly
*1643–1649 AD · Reformed Council*

> English Parliament's assembly that produced the Westminster Confession of Faith, Larger and Shorter Catechisms, Directory for Public Worship. The definitive statement of Reformed/Presbyterian theology. Still the confessional standard for Presbyterian and Reformed churches worldwide.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Westminster_Assembly)

## Doctrine Resolved
**Comprehensive Reformed systematic theology — Scripture, covenant, salvation, church**

## Key Participants
- John Owen (influenced it)
- Samuel Rutherford

# Second Vatican Council (Vatican II)
*1962–1965 AD · Catholic Ecumenical Council*

> Aggiornamento (bringing up to date). Liturgy in vernacular, ecumenism affirmed, religious liberty, salvation possible outside visible church, Scripture and Tradition reconceived (Dei Verbum). Opened Catholic-Protestant dialogue. Transformed Catholic engagement with modernity. Henri Nouwen, Gustavo Gutiérrez, Balthasar all shaped by it.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Second_Vatican_Council)

## Doctrine Resolved
**Church's relationship to modernity, ecumenism, religious liberty, liturgy reform**

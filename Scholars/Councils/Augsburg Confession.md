# Augsburg Confession
*1530 AD · Lutheran Confession*

> Philip Melanchthon's statement of Lutheran theology presented to Charles V. Defines Lutheran doctrines of justification, church, sacraments. Foundation document of Lutheranism. Still the primary confession of Lutheran churches worldwide.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Augsburg_Confession)

# Council of Carthage
*397 AD · Regional Council*

> Ratified the biblical canon established at Hippo (393). Affirmed 27 NT books plus OT including deuterocanon. The most influential canon decision before Trent. Jerome's Vulgate based on similar lists. This is the canon Luther later challenged.

# First Vatican Council
*1869–1870 AD · Catholic Ecumenical Council*

> Defined papal infallibility (ex cathedra definitions on faith and morals). Affirmed against rationalism and liberalism. Prompted Old Catholic schism. Defines Catholic ecclesiology that Vatican II would later re-contextualize.

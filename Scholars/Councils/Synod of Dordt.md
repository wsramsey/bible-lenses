# Synod of Dordt
*1618–1619 AD · Reformed Council*

> Dutch Reformed council that condemned Arminianism and formulated TULIP (Total Depravity, Unconditional Election, Limited Atonement, Irresistible Grace, Perseverance of Saints). The definitive Calvinist confession. Wesley and Arminians react against it. The Calvinist-Arminian fault line is defined here.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Synod_of_Dort)

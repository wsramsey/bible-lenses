# First Council of Nicaea
*325 AD · Ecumenical Council*

> Called by Constantine. 318 bishops. Condemned Arianism (Arius: Son is a created being). Formulated the Nicene Creed (homoousios — same substance as Father). Settled Easter dating. Foundation of Trinitarian orthodoxy. Everything after is either defending or attacking Nicaea.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/First_Council_of_Nicaea)

## Doctrine Resolved
**Trinity — full divinity of Christ condemned Arianism**

## Key Participants
- Athanasius of Alexandria
- Arius
- Constantine I

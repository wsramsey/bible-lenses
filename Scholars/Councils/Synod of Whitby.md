# Synod of Whitby
*664 AD · Regional Synod*

> English church decided to follow Roman (not Celtic/Columban) Easter calculation and Roman tonsure. Effectively brought English Christianity into Roman orbit and away from Irish/Celtic tradition. Landmark moment in Celtic vs Roman Christianity.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Synod_of_Whitby)

## Doctrine Resolved
**Easter dating — Roman vs Celtic practice in England**

- Wilfrid of York
- Colman of Lindisfarne

# Council of Hippo
*393 AD · Regional Council*

> First church council to list the 27-book NT canon as we have it today. Also affirmed OT canon including deuterocanonicals. Augustine attended. Ratified at Carthage (397 AD). Critical moment in canonization process.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Synod_of_Hippo)

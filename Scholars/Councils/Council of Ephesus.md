# Council of Ephesus
*431 AD · Ecumenical Council*

> Condemned Nestorius (who refused Theotokos — Mary as God-bearer, preferring Christotokos). Affirmed Mary as Theotokos. Split off Nestorian/Church of the East. Shaped Marian theology in Catholicism and Orthodoxy. Augustine died as this council was being called.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Council_of_Ephesus)

## Doctrine Resolved
**Theotokos — unity of Christ's person against Nestorianism**

## Key Participants
- Cyril of Alexandria
- Nestorius

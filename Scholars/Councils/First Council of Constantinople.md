# First Council of Constantinople
*381 AD · Ecumenical Council*

> Expanded Nicene Creed to include full divinity of the Holy Spirit (against Macedonians/Pneumatomachi). 'Niceno-Constantinopolitan Creed' — the Creed still used today in liturgy worldwide. Gregory of Nazianzus presided briefly. Established Constantinople's authority.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/First_Council_of_Constantinople)

## Doctrine Resolved
**Full divinity of the Holy Spirit — completed Trinitarian doctrine**

## Key Participants
- Gregory of Nazianzus
- Gregory of Nyssa

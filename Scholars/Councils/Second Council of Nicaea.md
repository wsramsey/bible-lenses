# Second Council of Nicaea
*787 AD · Ecumenical Council*

> Restored veneration of icons against Iconoclasm. Distinction between veneration (proskynesis) and worship (latreia). Accepted as 7th Ecumenical Council by Catholic and Orthodox but not most Protestants. John of Damascus's theology on icons was decisive.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Second_Council_of_Nicaea)

## Doctrine Resolved
**Permissibility of icons — against Iconoclasm**

## Key Participants
- John of Damascus (theology behind it)
- Empress Irene

# Council of Trent
*1545–1563 AD · Catholic Ecumenical Council*

> Catholic Counter-Reformation response to Protestantism. Affirmed: Latin Vulgate as authoritative, Tradition alongside Scripture, seven sacraments, justification by faith AND works, purgatory, indulgences (with reform), deuterocanonical books. Defined Catholic-Protestant divide that persists today.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Council_of_Trent)

**Catholic doctrine vs Protestant Reformation on Scripture, justification, sacraments, canon**

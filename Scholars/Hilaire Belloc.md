# Hilaire Belloc
*1870–1953 · Catholic · History & Apologetics*

> **Why he's here:** The other half of the "Chesterbelloc" — Chesterton's closest intellectual ally. Belloc was the historian and polemicist; Chesterton the literary genius. Together they defined early 20th-century Catholic intellectual culture in England. His famous line: "The Faith is Europe and Europe is the Faith."

## Key Concepts
- **Distributism** — economic alternative to both capitalism and socialism; property widely distributed; with Chesterton, the founding theorists
- **Europe is the Faith** — Western civilization is constitutively Catholic; secularism is parasitic on Christian capital
- **Historical revisionism** — challenged Protestant versions of Reformation history; argued the Reformation was about property seizure

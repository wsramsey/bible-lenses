*1922–1988 · Christian · Post-liberal / Yale School*

> **Theological lean:** Narrative identity of Jesus, Scripture's plain sense, Against historical-critical reduction

## Hermeneutical Lenses

- [[Lens - Post-liberal]] — [Post-liberal](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Postliberal_theology)
  *Church's grammar shapes meaning; against foundationalism; Lindbeck, Frei, Hauerwas*
- [[Lens - Narrative / Literary]] — [Narrative / Literary](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Narrative_theology)
  *Scripture as story; literary analysis of plot, character; Alter, Frei*
- [[Lens - Canonical Criticism]] — [Canonical Criticism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canonical_criticism)

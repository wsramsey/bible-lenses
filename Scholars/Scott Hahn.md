# Scott Hahn
*1957– · Catholic · Biblical Covenant Theology*

> **Why he's here:** The most influential Catholic apologist of the late 20th–early 21st century in American popular culture. A former Presbyterian minister who converted to Catholicism, Hahn's audio tapes of his conversion story circulated among thousands of Protestant-to-Catholic converts in the 1990s. His *The Lamb's Supper* connected Revelation and the Mass in a way that resonated with a mass audience.

## Key Concepts
- **Covenant** — the organizing principle of Scripture; God's covenant relationships (Adamic, Noahic, Abrahamic, Mosaic, Davidic, New) tell the single biblical story
- **The Mass as heaven on earth** — the Eucharistic liturgy is the fulfillment of the Revelation's heavenly worship; *The Lamb's Supper* thesis
- **Family of God** — the Trinity as family; the church as participation in Trinitarian family life
- **Fourth cup** — Jesus didn't drink the fourth Passover cup at the Last Supper; he drank it on the cross

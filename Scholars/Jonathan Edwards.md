# Jonathan Edwards
*1703–1758 · Christian · Reformed / New England Puritan*

> **Theological lean:** Religious Affections, Calvinist revivalism, Beauty of God

## Hermeneutical Lenses

- [[Lens - Redemptive-Historical]] — [Redemptive-Historical](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Redemptive-historical_hermeneutics)
  *Reads OT as foreshadowing fulfilled in Christ; Vos, Ridderbos*
- [[Lens - Typological / Figural]] — [Typological / Figural](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Typology_(theology))
  *OT persons/events as types fulfilled in NT; von Rad, Farrer*
- [[Lens - Literal / Grammatical]] — [Literal / Grammatical](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Biblical_literalism)
  *Plain meaning of words in grammar and syntax; Reformation method*

## Shaped By

- [[John Calvin]] — *foundational*
  > Edwards is Reformed Calvinist; reads Calvin via Westminster tradition

## Influenced

- [[John Piper]] — *direct*
  > Piper's Christian Hedonism is self-consciously Edwardsean
- [[R.C. Sproul]] — *formative*
  > Sproul wrote extensively on Edwards
- [[Tim Keller]] — *direct*
  > Keller's theology of joy and beauty is consciously Edwardsean

## Primary Works

- *Religious Affections*
- *Freedom of the Will*
- *The End for Which God Created the World*

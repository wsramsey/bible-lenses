# Skye Jethani
*1976– · Evangelical · Cultural Critique*

> **Why he's here:** Co-host of *The Holy Post* podcast (with Phil Vischer), one of the most listened-to evangelical commentary platforms. His *With* (2011) offered a taxonomy of distorted relationships with God (life over/under/from/for God) that resonated widely in post-evangelical conversations.

## Key Concepts
- **With** — the fifth way: life *with* God, not just using God or serving under him; relational union as the paradigm
- **Consumerism and the church** — the church has absorbed consumer culture; spiritual consumerism is the primary threat to formation
- **Institutional critique** — consistent critic of evangelical celebrity and institutional Christianity

## Primary Works
- *With* (2011)
- *The Divine Commodity* (2009)
- *What If Jesus Was Serious?* (2021)

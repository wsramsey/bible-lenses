# Rod Dreher
*1967– · Eastern Orthodox · The Benedict Option*

> **Why he's here:** The most widely read conservative Christian cultural critic of the 2010s–2020s. His *The Benedict Option* (2017) argued that Western Christianity is losing the culture war and should focus on building thick, intentional communities on the Benedictine model rather than fighting political battles. His trajectory — evangelical → Catholic → Orthodox — tracks a significant current in post-Protestant Christianity.

## Key Concepts
- **The Benedict Option** — named after St. Benedict; Christians should withdraw from culture war politics and invest in communal formation, liturgy, and Christian education as cultural survival strategy
- **Liquid modernity** — Western culture has dissolved the structures (family, community, religion) necessary for human flourishing
- **The long march through the institutions is over** — Christians have lost the cultural institutions; regroup rather than reconquer
- **Soft totalitarianism** — *Live Not By Lies*; social credit and corporate pressure are creating a new non-violent totalitarianism

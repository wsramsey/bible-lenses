# John MacArthur
*1939– · Evangelical · Expository Preaching*

> **Why he's here:** The most influential cessationist Reformed pastor-teacher of the late 20th–21st century. Pastor of Grace Community Church (Sun Valley, CA) since 1969. His verse-by-verse expository preaching through entire books of the Bible, distributed free through Grace to You, has reached tens of millions. His *The MacArthur Study Bible* (28 million copies) is the most widely used evangelical study Bible.

## Key Concepts
- **Expository preaching** — the sermon's structure and content governed by the text, not the preacher's topics; every verse of every book
- **Cessationism** — the miraculous gifts (tongues, prophecy, healing) ceased with the apostolic age; against the charismatic movement
- **Lordship salvation** — saving faith necessarily includes submission to Christ as Lord; against easy believism and "carnal Christian" theology
- **Biblical inerrancy** — strong inerrancy; Scripture is without error in all it affirms including science and history

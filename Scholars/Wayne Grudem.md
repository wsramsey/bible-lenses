# Wayne Grudem
*1948– · Evangelical · Systematic Theology*

> **Why he's here:** His *Systematic Theology* (1994) is the most widely used evangelical systematic theology in seminaries and churches — a genuinely comprehensive, readable, and persuasive presentation of Reformed evangelical doctrine. For millions of evangelical students, Grudem is the systematic theology.

## Key Concepts
- **Inerrancy** — Scripture is without error in everything it affirms; the Chicago Statement on Biblical Inerrancy (which he helped draft) is his benchmark
- **Complementarianism** — men and women are equal in dignity but have different roles; he co-founded the Council on Biblical Manhood and Womanhood
- **Eternal subordination of the Son** — his controversial position (since retracted/modified) that the Son is eternally subordinate in function to the Father within the Trinity, used to support gender complementarianism
- **Spiritual gifts** — "open but cautious" continuationism; spiritual gifts including prophecy continue today

## Primary Works
- *Systematic Theology* (1994)
- *Bible Doctrine* (1999, abridged)
- *Recovering Biblical Manhood and Womanhood* (1991, co-edited with John Piper)

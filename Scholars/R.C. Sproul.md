# R.C. Sproul
*1939–2017 · Christian · Reformed / Westminster Confessional*

> **Theological lean:** Classical Reformed, Inerrancy, Ligonier Ministries, Holiness of God

## Hermeneutical Lenses

- [[Lens - Literal / Grammatical]] — [Literal / Grammatical](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Biblical_literalism)
  *Plain meaning of words in grammar and syntax; Reformation method*
- [[Lens - Covenantal]] — [Covenantal](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Covenant_theology)
  *Reads Scripture through covenant of works/grace; Westminster tradition*
- [[Lens - Apologetic]] — [Apologetic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Christian_apologetics)
  *Defense of Christian faith against objections; Lewis, Schaeffer, McGrath*

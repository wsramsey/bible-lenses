# Tish Harrison Warren
*1981– · Anglican Evangelical · Liturgical Ordinary*

> **Why she's here:** Her *Liturgy of the Ordinary* (2016) became a touchstone for a generation of evangelicals discovering Anglican liturgical practice. Warren's writing sits at the intersection of evangelical spirituality and high-church Anglican formation, arguing that the ordinary rhythms of daily life are the primary site of spiritual formation.

## Key Concepts
- **Liturgy of the ordinary** — every mundane act (making the bed, eating breakfast) is a liturgical act; formation happens in repetition
- **Ordinary time** — the church calendar's longest season is a theology; most of life is not mountaintop; faithfulness is ordinary
- **Embodied formation** — the body is the site of spiritual life; practices precede and shape beliefs

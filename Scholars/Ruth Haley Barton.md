# Ruth Haley Barton
*1956– · Evangelical · Leadership Formation*

> **Why she's here:** Founder of the Transforming Center and the most influential evangelical voice on spiritual formation for leaders. Barton bridges the contemplative tradition (particularly [[Henri Nouwen]] and [[Thomas Merton]]) and evangelical ministry culture. Her *Strengthening the Soul of Your Leadership* addresses the spiritual formation of leaders as distinct from general congregational formation.

## Key Concepts
- **The leader's soul** — leadership failure is ultimately a soul problem; spiritual formation must precede and sustain leadership capacity
- **Solitude and silence** — the practice most essential for leaders and most neglected; without it, ministry operates from depletion
- **Transforming community** — small groups designed for transformation, not information; the format of deep spiritual formation
- **Discernment** — the communal and individual practice of attending to God's guidance

## Shaped By

# Hebrew Bible / Tanakh
*24 books · Jewish · Finalized: c. 90 AD (Council of Jamnia — disputed) / process earlier*

> The Jewish Scriptures in three parts: Torah (5), Nevi'im/Prophets (8), Ketuvim/Writings (11). 24 books by Jewish counting (= Protestant 39 by different divisions). This is the canon Jesus read. The Septuagint (LXX) adds books the Tanakh doesn't include.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hebrew_Bible)

## Disputed Books
None in the Tanakh itself — but Song of Songs, Ecclesiastes, and Esther were debated

## Theological Impact
The baseline. Every subsequent canon is a decision about what to do with this.

## Key Canon Decisions
- **Council of Jamnia (c. 90 AD)** — Rabbinic consolidation after Temple destruction — standardized Hebrew canon

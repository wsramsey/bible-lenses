# Peter Kreeft
*1937– · Catholic · Apologetics & Thomism*

> **Why he's here:** The most prolific Catholic popular philosopher-apologist of the late 20th century. Boston College professor with 80+ books. His *Handbook of Christian Apologetics* (with Ronald Tacelli) and his Socratic dialogues make philosophy and apologetics accessible to undergraduates and curious laypeople.

## Key Concepts
- **Socratic method** — philosophy as dialogue; his Socratic dialogues put Aquinas, Augustine, and Kreeft in conversation with modernity
- **Natural law** — moral truth accessible to reason; foundational for public discourse
- **Ecumenism** — unusual for a Catholic apologist; advocates Protestant-Catholic cooperation against secularism
- **Suffering** — his *Making Sense Out of Suffering* is one of the better popular theodicies

# Charles Stanley
*1932–2023 · Evangelical · Television Ministry*

> **Why he's here:** Founder of In Touch Ministries, former president of the Southern Baptist Convention. His television and radio ministry reached over 100 countries. His consistent pastoral emphasis on hearing from God and eternal security shaped millions of Southern Baptist laypeople.

## Key Concepts
- **Eternal security** — "once saved, always saved"; one of the most prominent defenders of this position in popular evangelicalism
- **Listening to God** — his best-known devotional theme; God communicates personally; how to cultivate sensitivity to the Holy Spirit
- **The blessing of obedience** — practical consequences of following or disobeying God

## Primary Works
- *How to Listen to God* (1985)
- *The Blessings of Brokenness* (1997)
- *In Touch* devotional series

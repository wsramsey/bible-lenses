# Rick Warren
*1954– · Evangelical · Purpose-Driven*

> **Why he's here:** *The Purpose Driven Life* (2002) is the best-selling hardback non-fiction book in history — 50 million copies. Founder of Saddleback Church (Lake Forest, CA), which became the model for seeker-sensitive megachurch ministry worldwide. Warren's influence on global pastoral practice is enormous even if his theological contribution is modest.

## Key Concepts
- **Five purposes** — every church exists for worship, fellowship, discipleship, ministry, and mission; the "purpose driven" framework
- **Seeker-sensitive** — Sunday services designed for unchurched people; theological depth in midweek settings
- **PEACE plan** — global initiative: plant churches, equip leaders, assist the poor, care for the sick, educate the next generation
- **Life purpose** — "You were made for God's purposes, not your own"; Christological telos made accessible

## Primary Works
- *The Purpose Driven Church* (1995)
- *The Purpose Driven Life* (2002)

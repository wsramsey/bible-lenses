# John Howard Yoder
*1927–1997 · Christian · Anabaptist / Peace Theology*

> **Theological lean:** Pacifism, Politics of Jesus, Christocentric ethics, Church as contrast society

## Hermeneutical Lenses

- [[Lens - Narrative / Literary]] — [Narrative / Literary](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Narrative_theology)
  *Scripture as story; literary analysis of plot, character; Alter, Frei*
- [[Lens - Liberation Hermeneutics]] — [Liberation Hermeneutics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Liberation_theology)
  *Reads from underside of history; poor, marginalized; Gutiérrez, Cone*
- [[Lens - Social-Scientific Criticism]] — [Social-Scientific Criticism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Social-scientific_criticism_of_the_Bible)
  *Applies anthropology and sociology; Malina, Neyrey*

## Shaped By

- [[Menno Simons]] — *foundational*
  > Yoder is heir to Menno's Anabaptist peaceable church tradition
- [[Karl Barth]] — *formative*
  > Yoder studied under Barth in Basel; Christocentrism shared
- [[Dietrich Bonhoeffer]] — *formative*
  > Bonhoeffer's Cost of Discipleship deeply shaped Yoder's costly nonviolence

## Influenced

- [[Stanley Hauerwas]] — *direct*
  > Hauerwas calls Yoder the greatest theologian of the 20th century; pacifism and ecclesiology

## Primary Works

- *The Politics of Jesus*
- *The Royal Priesthood*

# Jonathan Leeman
*1973– · Evangelical · Ecclesiology*

> **Why he's here:** Editorial director of 9Marks and the most prolific contemporary evangelical writer on ecclesiology and political theology. His work on church discipline, church membership, and the political witness of the church (especially *Political Church* and *How the Nations Rage*) represents the most serious Reformed evangelical engagement with political theology in recent years.

## Key Concepts
- **The church as embassy** — the local church is an embassy of the coming kingdom, displaying its politics in the present
- **Church and state** — both are legitimate but distinct authorities; the church's political witness is primarily through its own ordered life, not partisan engagement
- **Church discipline** — the exercise of the keys (Matt 16, 18) is constitutive of the church; local churches have real spiritual authority
- **Political church** — the church is inherently political (not partisan) because it organizes human life around an alternative Lord

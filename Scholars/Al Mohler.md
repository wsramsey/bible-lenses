# Al Mohler
*1959– · Evangelical · Reformed Southern Baptist*

> **Why he's here:** President of The Southern Baptist Theological Seminary since 1993 — the most influential Southern Baptist institution — and the leading intellectual voice of conservative Southern Baptist resurgence. His daily *The Briefing* podcast (theology and current events) has shaped how conservative evangelicals think about public life for two decades.

## Key Concepts
- **Conservative resurgence** — the successful effort to return SBC seminaries to inerrancy and Reformed soteriology after liberal drift
- **Cultural engagement** — every cultural development has theological implications; Christianity and public life cannot be separated
- **Inerrancy** — the Chicago Statement is the floor; no compromise
- **Confessional recovery** — the Baptist Faith and Message as binding confessional standard

## Primary Works
- *He Is Not Silent* (2008)
- *Words from the Fire* (2009)
- *The Conviction to Lead* (2012)
- *The Briefing* (daily podcast)

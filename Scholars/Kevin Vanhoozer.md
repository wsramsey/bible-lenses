# Kevin Vanhoozer
*1957– · Christian · Reformed / Theological Interpretation*

> **Theological lean:** Speech-act hermeneutics, Theodramatics, Canonical-linguistic theology

## Hermeneutical Lenses

- [[Lens - Speech-Act Theory]] — [Speech-Act Theory](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Speech_act_theory)
  *Language as action; Wolterstorff, Vanhoozer applied to Scripture*
- [[Lens - Theological Interpretation]] — [Theological Interpretation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Theological_interpretation_of_Scripture)
  *Scripture read within church's life and doctrine; Vanhoozer, Treier*
- [[Lens - Post-liberal]] — [Post-liberal](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Postliberal_theology)
  *Church's grammar shapes meaning; against foundationalism; Lindbeck, Frei, Hauerwas*

## Shaped By

- [[Paul Ricoeur]] — *direct*
  > Vanhoozer's speech-act hermeneutics builds on and critiques Ricoeur extensively
- [[John D. Caputo]] — *critical-dialogue*
  > Vanhoozer's hermeneutics partly a response to Caputo's deconstructive reading

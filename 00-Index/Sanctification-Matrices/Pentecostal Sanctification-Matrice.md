# Pentecostal Sanctification Matrix

## What Sanctification Is
The holy transformation of the believer by the Spirit for Christlike life and mission.

## Primary Means
- prayer
- repentance
- Spirit baptism/filling
- scripture
- worship
- obedience

## Signs of Growth
- holiness
- bold witness
- spiritual vitality
- victory over sin
- love and power rightly ordered

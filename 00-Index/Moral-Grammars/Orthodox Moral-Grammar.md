# Orthodox Moral Grammar

## What Counts as Good
Participation in divine life through holiness, prayer, repentance, communion, and healing.

## What Counts as Evil
Corruption, alienation, passions, and distortion of the image of God.

## What Sin Is
A sickness and bondage that estranges the person from life in God.

## What Repentance Is
Metanoia: a reorientation of the person through prayer, confession, ascetic struggle, and grace.

## What Holiness Is
Transfiguration in Christ by grace.

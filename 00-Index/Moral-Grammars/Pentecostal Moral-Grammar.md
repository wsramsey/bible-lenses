# Pentecostal Moral Grammar

## What Counts as Good
Spirit-led holiness, obedience, witness, repentance, and expectancy before God.

## What Counts as Evil
Sin that quenches the Spirit, distorts holiness, enslaves the person, or blocks obedience and mission.

## What Sin Is
Bondage, rebellion, uncleanness, compromise, and resistance to God.

## What Repentance Is
A decisive turning to Christ marked by surrender, confession, faith, and transformation.

## What Holiness Is
A Spirit-filled life set apart for God in purity, power, and witness.

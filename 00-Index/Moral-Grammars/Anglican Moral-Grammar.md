# Anglican Moral Grammar

## What Counts as Good
Faithful communion with God expressed in worship, repentance, holiness, charity, and ordered common life.

## What Counts as Evil
Sin that distorts love of God and neighbor, disorders desire, and fractures communion.

## What Sin Is
Guilt, disorder, rebellion, and alienation from God requiring grace, repentance, and reformation of life.

## What Repentance Is
Turning again toward God in confession, trust, amendment of life, and prayer.

## What Holiness Is
A life formed by prayer, sacrament, scripture, virtue, and ordinary faithfulness.

## What Transformation Looks Like
Steady growth in charity, humility, worship, patience, and Christlike character.

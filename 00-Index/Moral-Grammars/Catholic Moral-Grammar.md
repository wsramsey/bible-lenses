# Catholic Moral Grammar

## What Counts as Good
Participation in divine life through grace, charity, virtue, worship, and communion with the Church.

## What Counts as Evil
Sin that wounds communion, distorts desire, and refuses God and neighbor.

## What Sin Is
A rupture in communion and a wound requiring repentance, grace, and healing.

## What Repentance Is
Contrition, confession, amendment, penance, and return to God and the Church.

## What Holiness Is
Growth in charity, virtue, sacramental life, and union with God.

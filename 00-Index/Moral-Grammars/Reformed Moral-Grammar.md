# Reformed Moral Grammar

## What Counts as Good
Faithful obedience flowing from grace and directed to the glory of God.

## What Counts as Evil
Idolatry, rebellion, unbelief, injustice, self-rule, and any refusal of God's lordship.

## What Sin Is
Guilt, corruption, bondage, and rebellion before a holy God.

## What Repentance Is
Turning from sin to Christ in faith, sorrow, obedience, and renewed dependence.

## What Holiness Is
Spirit-wrought conformity to Christ expressed in obedience, worship, and love.

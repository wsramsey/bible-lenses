# Theosis

## Core Question
What does it mean for human beings to participate in the divine life without becoming identical with God?

## Why It Matters
Theosis frames salvation as transformation, communion, and participation rather than only pardon.

## Major Positions
- deification by grace
- participation in divine energies/life
- sanctification read through patristic ontology

## Hermeneutic Variance
It thrives where biblical interpretation is sacramental, participatory, and figural rather than narrowly forensic.

## Tradition-Specific Readings
Strongest in Eastern Christianity, but with analogues in Catholic and some Protestant retrieval traditions.

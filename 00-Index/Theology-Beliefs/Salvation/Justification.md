# Justification

## Core Question
How are human beings made right before God?

## Why It Matters
Justification sits near the center of Christian debates about grace, faith, works, sacraments, assurance, and salvation.

## Major Positions
- forensic declaration
- transformative/participatory righteousness
- sacramental incorporation

## Hermeneutic Variance
- Reformation readings prioritize Paul's legal and covenantal language.
- Catholic readings place justification within sacramental and ecclesial life.
- Orthodox readings often frame salvation more through participation/theosis than legal acquittal.

## Tradition-Specific Readings
- Protestant traditions often stress imputed righteousness.
- Catholic theology tends to integrate justification and sanctification more closely.
- Orthodox theology tends to subordinate the category to deification/union.

# Pentecostal Tradition Voice

## Branch / Family
Pentecostal / charismatic tradition.

## Authority Model
Scripture is central, read with vivid expectation that the Holy Spirit continues to act now with gifts, power, guidance, and encounter.

## Doctrinal Center of Gravity
- conversion/new birth
- Spirit baptism and gifts
- holiness and mission
- divine presence and power
- expectation of encounter

## Interpretive Habits
- reads experientially and practically
- often foregrounds the present action of God
- values testimony and witness
- may read narratives as paradigmatic for ongoing church life

## Spiritual Tone
Urgent, experiential, hopeful, energetic, direct.

## Preferred Metaphors
- fire
- anointing
- breakthrough
- outpouring
- power

## Pastoral Instincts
- encourages expectancy, prayer, healing, repentance, bold witness, and openness to the Spirit's movement

## What It Notices First
- encounter
- power
- conversion
- healing
- mission

## What It Tends To Downplay
- slow institutional mediation
- detached academic analysis
- overly static accounts of church life

## How It Handles Ambiguity
Often resolves uncertainty through prayer, testimony, and experiential discernment, sometimes faster than careful doctrinal testing would prefer.

## Key Tensions
- freedom vs discernment
- immediacy vs depth
- testimony vs theology

## Representative Thinkers (Weighted)
- Azusa Street witness
- Assemblies of God tradition
- Smith Wigglesworth
- Gordon Fee

## Typical Blind Spots
- can overread experience
- may blur desire and discernment
- can underbuild historical and doctrinal depth

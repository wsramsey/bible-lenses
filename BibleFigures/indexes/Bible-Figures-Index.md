# Bible Figures Index

## Purpose
Master index for Bible figure research and canonical figure pages.

## Folder Roles
- `raw/` — OCR, PDFs, article captures, source dumps
- `compiled/` — extracted summaries, scholar positions, draft figure material
- `canonical/` — stable figure pages
- `generated/` — query outputs, study packets, timelines, reports
- `indexes/` — navigational maps
- `health-check/` — missing data, duplicates, broken-link, and coverage reports

## Required Sections for Canonical Figure Pages
- Biblical role
- Primary texts
- Narrative significance
- Key themes
- History of interpretation
- Denominational/traditional readings
- Use in art, preaching, doctrine, politics, or controversy
- Related scholars / sources
- Open questions / gaps

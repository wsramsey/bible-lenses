# Atonement

## Core Question
How does the life, death, and resurrection of Jesus reconcile humanity to God?

## Why It Matters
Atonement stands at the center of Christian interpretation of the cross, salvation, justice, mercy, evil, sacrifice, and divine love.

## Major Positions
- Christus Victor
- satisfaction
- penal substitution
- moral influence
- recapitulation

## Hermeneutic Variance
Different readings privilege sacrificial, legal, participatory, apocalyptic, or exemplarist themes.

## Tradition-Specific Readings
Patristic traditions often emphasize victory and participation; medieval Latin theology sharpened satisfaction; Protestant traditions often foreground penal or forensic emphases.

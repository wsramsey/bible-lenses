# Major Christian Movements — Complete Index

## Pre-Reformation Movements
- [[Monasticism]] — Desert Fathers through Benedictines, Franciscans, Dominicans
- [[Mysticism]] — Rhineland, English, Spanish, Orthodox
- [[Scholasticism]] — Anselm through Aquinas; faith and reason
- [[Catharism]] — dualist heresy; Albigensian Crusade
- [[Waldensian Movement]] — poverty reform; survived to join Reformation
- [[Devotio Moderna]] — Thomas à Kempis; practical piety
- [[Conciliarism]] — councils over popes; resolved the Schism

## Reformation Movements (16th century)
- [[Lutheranism]] — grace, faith, Scripture
- [[Reformed / Calvinist Movement]] — sovereignty of God; Geneva
- [[Anabaptism]] — radical reformation; believer's baptism; nonviolence
- [[Anglicanism]] — the English via media
- [[The Counter-Reformation / Catholic Reform]]

## Post-Reformation (17th–18th century)
- [[Pietism]] — Spener, Zinzendorf; heart religion
- [[Puritanism]] — English/American; covenant theology
- [[Quietism]] — Fénelon, Guyon; pure love; condemned
- [[Jansenism]] — Augustinian rigorism; condemned

## Revivals and Awakenings (18th–19th century)
- [[Methodism]] — John Wesley; practical holiness
- [[The Great Awakenings]] — American revivalism
- [[The Evangelical Movement]] — transdenominational; conversionism
- [[The Holiness Movement]] — Wesleyan sanctification
- [[The Oxford Movement]] — Anglican Catholic retrieval; Newman

## Modern Movements (20th–21st century)
- [[Pentecostalism]] — Azusa Street; tongues; healing
- [[The Charismatic Renewal]] — Pentecostal gifts in mainline churches
- [[Liberation Theology]] — Latin American; poor as locus of God
- [[Neo-Orthodoxy]] — Barth; recovery of Reformation doctrine
- [[Fundamentalism]] — inerrancy; against liberalism
- [[Neo-Evangelicalism]] — Carl Henry; Billy Graham; responsible engagement
- [[The Emerging/Emergent Church]] — postmodern engagement
- [[The Liturgical Renewal]] — Vatican II; ecumenical retrieval
- [[The New Monasticism]] — communities of practice; Shane Claiborne
- [[The Benedict Option]] — Dreher; withdrawal and community
- [[Third Wave / Signs and Wonders]] — Wimber; power evangelism
- [[Word of Faith / Prosperity Gospel]] — health and wealth
- [[The New Apostolic Reformation]] — spiritual warfare; apostles/prophets
- [[Progressive Christianity]] — open theology; deconstruction
- [[Christian Nationalism]] — America as Christian nation

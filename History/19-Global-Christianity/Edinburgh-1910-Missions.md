# Edinburgh World Missionary Conference (1910)
*"The evangelization of the world in this generation"*

The most important missionary conference in history. 1,200 delegates from Protestant missions worldwide. Their slogan: "The evangelization of the world in this generation."

Edinburgh 1910 produced:
- The modern ecumenical movement (leading to the World Council of Churches, 1948)
- Missiology as an academic discipline
- The recognition that Christianity's future was global, not Western

The conference included almost no African, Asian, or Latin American delegates — an irony, given that 20th century Christianity was shaped by exactly those regions.
